I'm a student in Webdesign at Caen. With a sense of Art, i want to focus on the UI/UX Design of the Web and App.

I've started with computers games, with the interest to know how was developped my favorites games. Then I started to do my studies after my baccalaureate. I entered the University of Caen in Computer Science, without really knowing where to go. I'm now at MyDigitalSchool for at least 3 years.

As I practice music more and more intensely in my free time, I thought about merging these two interests and using my artistic sense to complement my computer skills. From now on, I want to pursue studies in UI / UX designer, Front End developpement or mobile development.

Now, i'm creating complex illustrations and design for differents communities, orders and work.
